Company Introduction

YUDO specializes in Hot Runner system, which is the core components of mold, and manufactures all essential parts internally including nozzle, Manifold, Mold Base, Heater, and so on.

YUDO manages excellent quality management system through in-house R&D, Design, Manufacturing, Assembly, and Customer Service.

It realizes Smart Factory for customer satisfaction with optimizing the overall production process and maximizing efficiency through Integrated Monitoring & Controlling/Business Management System.

With a total of 139 global service networks, YUDO has been making every effort to respond quickly to customer requests.
