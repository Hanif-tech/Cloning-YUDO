Automobile

With many years of accumulated experience and comprehensive patented technologies, YUDO continues to meet customers' needs in injecting automotive interior, exterior and engine components.

YUDO's self-developed TINA AM system for automotive molds is easy to assemble and disassemble, solves worries for customers and improves customer's market competitiveness.

Automobile

  • Completely pre-assembled and pre-wired for easy installation.
  • Screw-in nozzle, integrated leak-proof protection.
  • Pneumatic & hydraulic cylinder drive, separate cooling.

Advantages

  • Independent control( without Tablet).

  • Quick colour change function.

  • Independent WIFI Network to communicate
