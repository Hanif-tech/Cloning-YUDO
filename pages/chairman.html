Chairman's Greetings

Yujin Yu , the third chairman of YUDO (Term of office:2009 ~)

Dear Customers and YUDO Family Since being found in 1980 by chairman Francis Yu and grown under the leadership of chairman Tommy Lee, YUDO now marks its 40th anniversary in 2020. As Francis said, YUDO was able to become the number one Hot Runner company in the global market thanks to the support from our customers and dedication of more than 3,000 YUDO Family. While succeeding the former chairman’s philosophy, I would like to pursuit a new challenge of making YUDO a company lasting more than a century under the slogan “Vision 100 Years YUDO”. The core values of this vision can be summarized to “Customer focus”, “Cutting-edge technology realizing customer’s dream”, and “Company culture respected by customers.” We would like you to look forward for YUDO’s future endeavor, who’s entire business philosophy is focused on the customer.
